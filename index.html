The Story Of Ben

In a small town that mirrors the entire world, a boy named Ben once lived in harmony with nature. Every day, Ben loved exploring the outdoors—playing under the tall, green trees, breathing the fresh air, and listening to the birds sing. His town was full of life, and it was perfect.


But recently, things started changing. The sky, once bright and blue, is now often filled with dark clouds of smoke. The clean river where Ben once played is clogged with trash, and the animals he used to see have disappeared. Buildings and factories are rising quickly, and with them, new problems are appearing daily—pollution, deforestation, and waste.


Ben knows he can’t save the town alone, so he needs YOUR help! Together with Ben, you’ll embark on an epic quest to fix the problems plaguing his town and restore its natural beauty.
