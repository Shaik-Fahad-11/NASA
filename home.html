Story Line

Chapter 1

Ben notices that the forests are being cleared to make way for a new factory. While replanting trees seems like an easy solution, he realizes that biodiversity in the forest is more important than just planting random trees. A local logging company offers to help, but they want to plant only one type of fast-growing tree to quickly replace the lost forests.

Chapter 2

Ben’s town faces an energy crisis. The town’s growing population has caused energy consumption to skyrocket, and the local power plant, which relies heavily on fossil fuels, is struggling to keep up.

Chapter 3

Ben understands that replanting the forest requires community involvement, but the townspeople are not motivated because they don’t see immediate benefits.

Chapter 4

The air quality in Ben’s town has worsened dramatically. Factories, vehicles, and construction activities are constantly pumping pollutants into the atmosphere.

Finale

As the effects of climate change intensify, the player is presented with a final set of critical choices, each of which will directly impact the town’s future. There are three potential endings based on the overall points the player has accumulated throughout the game.
